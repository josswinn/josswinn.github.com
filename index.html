Hello World

I live in Lincoln, UK with my wife and daughter. I work at the University of Lincoln in the Centre for Educational Research and Development and co-ordinate the work of LNCD. I help develop software and publish, too. I'm interested in hacking as labour and how hackers produce value. I'm especially interested in hacking as a craft and how young hackers learn their craft.

I am a founding member of the Social Science Centre, Lincoln, and between 2009-11, ran WriteToReply with Tony Hirst. My work blog is Expedient Means.
