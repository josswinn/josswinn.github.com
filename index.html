Hello World

I live in Lincoln, UK with my wife and daughter. I work at the University of Lincoln in the Centre for Educational Research and Development and co-ordinate the work of LNCD. I help develop software and publish, too. I'm interested in hacking as labour and how hackers produce value. I'm especially interested in hacking as a craft and how young hackers learn their craft. Outside of my work, I enjoy building, maintaining and riding bikes.
