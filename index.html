About

I'm interested in critical political economy, energy, the Internet, peer production, fixed gear bicycles and bread.

I enjoy the films of Terrence Malick and Chris Marker, music by the Pixies, and the books of Cormac McCarthy.

My work blog is Expedient Means. I collect links, quotes and other people's ideas on Elsewhere.

I am a founding member of the Social Science Centre, Lincoln, and between 2009-11, ran WriteToReply with Tony Hirst.
